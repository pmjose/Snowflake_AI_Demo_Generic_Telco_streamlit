# SnowTelco 5G Network Expansion Report 2025

## Executive Summary

2025 marked a significant year in SnowTelco's 5G journey. We expanded coverage from 65% to 72% of the UK population, activating over 600 new sites and achieving major milestones in network performance.

## Technology Milestones

### Q1 2025
- Completed 3.5 GHz spectrum deployment in London
- Launched VoNR (Voice over New Radio) pilot
- Achieved 1 Gbps speeds in test locations

### Q2 2025
- Extended 5G to 10 new cities
- Deployed network slicing capability
- Upgraded core to 5G SA (Standalone)

### Q3 2025
- Activated 700 MHz for rural coverage
- Launched enterprise private 5G service
- Completed backhaul fibre to 500 sites

### Q4 2025
- Achieved 72% population coverage milestone
- Deployed edge computing in 3 locations
- Successfully handled Christmas traffic surge

---

## Challenges Faced

### Planning Delays
- 45 sites delayed due to local authority approvals
- Average delay: 4 months
- Mitigation: Enhanced pre-application engagement

### Supply Chain
- Chip shortages impacted Q2 deliveries
- Resolved by Q3 with alternative suppliers
- Buffer stock increased to 6 weeks

### Spectrum Interference
- Minor interference issues in 3 locations
- Resolved through coordination with adjacent operators
