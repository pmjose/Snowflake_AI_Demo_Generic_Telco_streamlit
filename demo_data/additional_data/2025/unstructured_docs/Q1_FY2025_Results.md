## Outlook

Q1 performance was in line with expectations. The typically slower post-Christmas period impacted subscriber growth, but retention improved. We expect acceleration in Q2 with new marketing campaigns.

**Key Q2 Priorities:**
1. Launch spring promotional campaign
2. Expand 5G to 10 additional cities
3. Roll out enhanced self-service app
4. Complete CRM migration Phase 1
