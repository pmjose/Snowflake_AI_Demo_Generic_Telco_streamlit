# SnowTelco Group PLC
## Annual Report & Accounts FY2025

**For the financial year ended 31 December 2025**

---

## Chairman's Statement

Dear Shareholders,

2025 has been a year of solid performance amid challenging market conditions. While we faced headwinds from increased competition and inflationary pressures, SnowTelco has maintained its market position and laid the groundwork for accelerated growth in 2026.

### Key Highlights FY2025

- **Revenue:** £478.2M (FY2024: £465.8M, +2.7%)
- **EBITDA:** £143.5M (30.0% margin)
- **Mobile Subscribers:** 927,000 (+4.2% YoY)
- **5G Coverage:** 72% of UK population
- **NPS Score:** +42 (industry average: +35)
- **Employee Count:** 2,850

### Strategic Progress

Our transformation programme made significant progress in 2025:

1. **Network Investment:** £85M invested in 5G infrastructure
2. **Digital First:** 62% of customer interactions now digital
3. **Partner Expansion:** 1,050+ channel partners
4. **Cost Efficiency:** £12M operational savings achieved

### Market Position

SnowTelco maintained its position as the UK's fifth-largest mobile operator:

| Operator | Market Share 2025 |
|----------|------------------|
| EE | 26.8% |
| Vodafone | 23.2% |
| Three | 19.5% |
| O2 | 18.8% |
| **SnowTelco** | **6.2%** |
| Others | 5.5% |

### Outlook for 2026

We enter 2026 with confidence. Key priorities include:

- Accelerate 5G rollout to 85% coverage
- Launch enhanced B2B offerings
- Achieve 1M subscriber milestone
- Improve NPS to +45
- Target revenue growth of 8-10%

**Sir William Thompson**
*Chairman*
*March 2026*

## Chief Executive's Review

### Operational Performance

FY2025 demonstrated resilience across our business segments:

**Consumer Division (70% of revenue)**
- Subscriber growth: +3.8%
- ARPU: £28.50 (down 2.1% due to competitive pricing)
- Churn rate: 1.8% monthly (improved from 2.0%)

**Business Division (30% of revenue)**
- SMB growth: +5.2%
- Enterprise growth: +4.8%
- Average contract value: £8,500/year
