## Q1 FY2026 Highlights

SnowTelco Group PLC announces a strong start to FY2026, with continued momentum across both B2B and Consumer Mobile segments.

| Metric | Q1 FY26 | Q1 FY25 | Growth |

## CEO Statement

**Sarah Chen, Chief Executive Officer, commented:**

"We have made an excellent start to FY2026, with strong momentum in both segments. Consumer mobile subscriber growth continues to exceed expectations, and we are confident of reaching our one million subscriber target ahead of schedule.

Our B2B business continues to win market share, with particularly strong performance in contact centre solutions. The investment we are making in AI capabilities positions us well for the next phase of growth.

We remain focused on executing our strategy and are reaffirming our full-year guidance."
