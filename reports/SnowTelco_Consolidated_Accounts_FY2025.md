## Notes to the Financial Statements

### Note 1: Accounting Policies

#### Basis of Preparation
The consolidated financial statements have been prepared in accordance with UK-adopted international accounting standards and the Companies Act 2006.

#### Going Concern
The Directors have assessed the Group's ability to continue as a going concern and are satisfied that the Group has adequate resources to continue in operational existence for at least 12 months from the date of approval of these financial statements.

#### Revenue Recognition
Revenue is recognised when control of goods or services is transferred to the customer:
- **Subscription revenue**: Recognised over the contract period
- **Usage revenue**: Recognised as services are consumed
- **Hardware revenue**: Recognised on delivery
- **Professional services**: Recognised as services are performed
