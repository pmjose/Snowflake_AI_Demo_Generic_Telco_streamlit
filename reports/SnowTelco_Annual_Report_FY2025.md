# SnowTelco Group PLC
## Annual Report & Consolidated Accounts FY2025
### For the Year Ended 31 March 2025

---

## Chairman's Statement

Dear Shareholders,

I am pleased to present SnowTelco Group's Annual Report for the financial year 2025. This has been a transformational year as we expanded our consumer mobile business while maintaining strong growth in our core B2B unified communications segment.

Our strategy of combining enterprise-grade UCaaS/CCaaS solutions with consumer mobile services has proven successful, creating a diversified telecommunications group well-positioned for the UK's digital future.

**Sir James Mitchell**  
*Chairman, SnowTelco Group PLC*

## Outlook for FY2026

The Board remains confident in the Group's prospects:

- **B2B**: Continued migration to cloud communications, strong pipeline in contact centre
- **Consumer Mobile**: Target 1 million subscribers by March 2026
- **5G**: Investment in network densification in urban centres
- **EBITDA**: Expect margin expansion of 0.5-1.0pp
- **Dividend**: Progressive dividend policy maintained
