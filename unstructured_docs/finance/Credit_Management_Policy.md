# Credit Management Policy
**SnowTelco Communications - Finance Operations**

## Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for credit assessment, monitoring, and collections for SnowTelco customers to minimise bad debt while supporting commercial growth.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- Consumer mobile customers
- B2B customers
- Channel partners
- All credit decisions and collections activities

---

## Credit Assessment

### Consumer Credit Scoring

#### Credit Check Process

1. **Application Received**
2. **Identity Verification**
3. **Credit Bureau Check** (Experian, Equifax, TransUnion)
4. **Internal Data Check** (previous accounts, fraud flags)
5. **Score Calculation**
6. **Decision and Limit**

#### Credit Tiers

| Tier | Score Range | Credit Limit | Deposit |
|------|-------------|--------------|---------|
| Premium | 750+ | £500 | None |
| Standard | 600-749 | £250 | None |
| Moderate | 450-599 | £100 | £50-100 |
| Secured | <450 | £50 | £100-200 |
| Declined | Various | - | - |

#### Decline Reasons

- Credit score below threshold
- Previous bad debt with SnowTelco
- Fraud indicators
- Identity verification failed
- Multiple recent applications

### B2B Credit Assessment

#### Assessment Criteria

| Factor | Weight | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Credit score (D&B, Experian) | 30% | Credit bureau |
| Payment history | 25% | Trade references |
| Financial statements | 20% | Accounts filing |
| Time in business | 15% | Companies House |
| Industry risk | 10% | Sector analysis |

#### Credit Limits

| Annual Revenue | Indicative Limit |
|----------------|------------------|
| <£1M | £5,000 |
| £1-5M | £25,000 |
| £5-20M | £100,000 |
| £20-100M | £250,000 |
| >£100M | £500,000+ |

#### Approval Authority

| Limit | Approval |
|-------|----------|
| <£25,000 | Credit Analyst |
| £25,000-£100,000 | Credit Manager |
| £100,000-£500,000 | Finance Director |
| >£500,000 | CFO |

## Credit Monitoring

### Consumer Monitoring

| Alert | Threshold | Action |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Usage spike | 150% of average | Review account |
| Credit limit approach | 75% | SMS warning |
| Credit limit breach | 100% | Outbound barring |
| Payment missed | 1 day overdue | Dunning start |

### B2B Monitoring

| Metric | Frequency | Action Threshold |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| Days Sales Outstanding | Monthly | >45 days |
| Credit utilisation | Weekly | >80% |
| Payment pattern | Monthly | Deterioration |
| Credit score change | Quarterly | -10% |

### Watchlist Criteria

Account placed on watchlist if:
- 2+ late payments in 6 months
- Bounced payment
- Significant credit score decline
- Industry distress
- Adverse news

## Bad Debt Management

### Write-Off Criteria

| Criteria | Timeline |
|----------|----------|
| All collection efforts exhausted | 180 days |
| Customer deceased | With death certificate |
| Customer bankrupt | With insolvency notice |
| Uneconomic to pursue | <£50 balance |
| Disputed (valid) | Post investigation |

### Write-Off Approval

| Amount | Approval |
|--------|----------|
| <£500 | Credit Manager |
| £500-£5,000 | Finance Director |
| £5,000-£25,000 | CFO |
| >£25,000 | Board |

### Debt Sale

- Aged debt (>180 days) considered for sale
- Minimum batch: £100,000
- Sale to licensed DCAs only
- Data protection compliance

## Dispute Management

### Dispute Process

1. **Receive** - Log dispute, pause collection
2. **Investigate** - Review billing, usage, complaints
3. **Resolve** - Adjust or uphold
4. **Communicate** - Inform customer
5. **Close** - Resume collection if appropriate

### Dispute SLAs

| Complexity | Resolution Target |
|------------|-------------------|
| Simple (billing query) | 5 business days |
| Medium (usage dispute) | 10 business days |
| Complex (contract dispute) | 20 business days |

## Security and Deposits

### When Required

- Credit score below threshold
- Payment arrangement default
- Previous bad debt (cleared)
- High-value equipment
- New business <2 years

### Deposit Terms

| Type | Amount | Return Conditions |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Consumer | £50-200 | 12 months good payment |
| B2B | 1-3 months revenue | Contract end |
| Equipment | Device value | Device return |

### Alternatives

- Bank guarantee
- Parent company guarantee
- Letter of credit
- Prepayment

## Compliance

### Regulatory Requirements

- FCA guidance on vulnerable customers
- Ofcom complaints handling
- Consumer Credit Act compliance
- GDPR data handling

### Vulnerable Customers

**Identification:**
- Age (elderly)
- Health issues disclosed
- Financial hardship
- Life events (bereavement, divorce)

**Treatment:**
- Extended payment terms
- Reduced collection intensity
- Specialist team handling
- Signposting to support services
