# Q3 FY2026 Financial Report
## SnowTelco Group PLC
### For the Quarter Ended 31 December 2025

**Prepared: January 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

Q3 FY2026 delivered exceptional performance with Group revenue of **£142.8m**, up 15.2% year-over-year. Consumer Mobile momentum continued with net subscriber additions of 28,450 in the quarter, bringing total mobile subscribers to **1,008,620** - surpassing our one million milestone ahead of schedule. Adjusted EBITDA reached **£30.1m** (21.1% margin), demonstrating operating leverage as we scale.

## Outlook

The Board is pleased with the exceptional Q3 performance and the achievement of the one million subscriber milestone. Consumer mobile momentum remains strong entering Q4, supported by:

- Continued 5G network expansion
- Successful holiday promotional campaigns
- Improving churn metrics
- Strong device sales pipeline

B2B business continues to benefit from cloud migration and AI feature adoption. The integration of AI capabilities across both UCaaS and CCaaS platforms is driving customer wins and improved unit economics.
