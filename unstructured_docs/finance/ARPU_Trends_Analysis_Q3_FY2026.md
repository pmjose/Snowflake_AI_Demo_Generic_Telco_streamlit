# ARPU Trends Analysis Q3 FY2026
**SnowTelco Group PLC - Revenue Per User Analysis**

**Prepared: January 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) trends across SnowTelco's Consumer Mobile and B2B segments for Q3 FY2026. With **1,008,620 mobile subscribers** (milestone achieved December 2025), SnowTelco demonstrates strong unit economics with improving ARPU trends. Blended mobile ARPU reached **£19.94** in Q3, up 16.2% year-over-year, driven by successful upselling to higher-value plans and increased 5G adoption.

### ARPU Drivers - Q3 FY26

**Positive Drivers:**
- 5G plan uptake: +4pp to 48% of base
- Premium plan migrations: +12,400 subscribers
- Device bundle attach rate: 62% (up from 58%)
- International roaming revenue: +18% vs Q2
- Average data usage: 18.2GB (+6% QoQ)

**Headwinds:**
- Competitive pricing pressure in value segment
- Family plan discount adoption: now 22% of base
- Seasonal promotional pricing (Black Friday, Christmas)

## ARPU Improvement Initiatives

### Consumer Mobile

1. **5G Upsell Programme**
   - Target: 55% 5G penetration by Q4
   - ARPU uplift: £4-6 per migrated subscriber
   - Q3 migrations: 38,400 subscribers

2. **Family Plan Expansion**
   - Average lines per family plan: 3.2
   - Family plan ARPU: £72.50 per plan
   - Churn reduction: 25% lower vs individual plans

3. **Value-Added Services**
   - Device insurance: 28% attach rate
   - Cloud storage: 15% attach rate
   - Entertainment bundles: 12% attach rate

### B2B

1. **AI Feature Monetisation**
   - AI agent assist premium: +£25/seat/month
   - Speech analytics: +£15/seat/month
   - Workforce management: +£10/seat/month

2. **Platform Expansion**
   - Teams integration driving seat growth
   - Webex Calling certification expanding TAM
   - Analytics dashboards driving retention

## Appendix: ARPU Calculation Methodology

- **Mobile ARPU:** Total mobile service revenue ÷ average subscribers in period
- **B2B ARPU:** Segment recurring revenue ÷ average active seats
- **Blended ARPU:** Total revenue ÷ total subscriber/seat count
- **Excludes:** One-time hardware revenue, professional services
