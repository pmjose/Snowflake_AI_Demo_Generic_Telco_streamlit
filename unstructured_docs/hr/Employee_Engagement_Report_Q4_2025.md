# SnowTelco Employee Engagement Report

## Highlights

### What's Working Well

1. **Hybrid Working Model (+0.5)**
   - 85% satisfaction with flexibility
   - Collaboration days well-received
   - Home office support appreciated

2. **Career Development Platform (+0.6)**
   - 1,200 employees created development plans
   - 45 internal moves in Q4
   - Mentorship program oversubscribed

3. **Leadership Transparency (+0.4)**
   - CEO monthly town halls rated 8.5/10
   - Strategy communication improved
   - "Ask Me Anything" sessions popular

4. **Team Collaboration (+0.3)**
   - Cross-functional projects increasing
   - Knowledge sharing platforms active
   - Team events resuming

### Areas for Improvement

1. **Compensation Clarity (-0.1 vs industry)**
   - Pay band visibility requested
   - Bonus criteria unclear to 35%
   - Market benchmarking questions

2. **Workload Management (7.1)**
   - 28% report high stress levels
   - Resource constraints in some teams
   - Project prioritization needed

3. **Technology Tools (7.0)**
   - Legacy system frustration
   - Integration issues reported
   - Training gap on new tools
