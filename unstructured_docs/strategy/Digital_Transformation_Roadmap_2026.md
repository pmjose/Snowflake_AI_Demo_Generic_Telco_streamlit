# SnowTelco Digital Transformation Roadmap 2026

## Executive Summary

This document outlines SnowTelco's digital transformation strategy for 2026, focusing on network modernization, customer experience enhancement, and operational efficiency through AI/ML adoption.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Network Modernization

#### 5G Standalone Deployment
- **Target:** 95% urban population coverage by Q4 2026
- **Investment:** £180M capital expenditure
- **Key Milestones:**
  - Q1 2026: Complete core network upgrade to 5G SA
  - Q2 2026: Launch 5G SA in London, Manchester, Birmingham
  - Q3 2026: Expand to 15 major cities
  - Q4 2026: Rural 5G fixed wireless access rollout

#### Open RAN Initiative
- Partner with Mavenir and Samsung for Open RAN deployment
- Target 30% of new sites on Open RAN by end of 2026
- Expected 25% reduction in RAN operational costs

### 2. Customer Experience Transformation

#### Digital-First Strategy
- **Self-Service Target:** 80% of transactions via digital channels
- **AI Chatbot Resolution:** 65% first-contact resolution rate
- **App Downloads Target:** 2M active users by December 2026

#### Omnichannel Experience
- Unified customer view across all touchpoints
- Real-time personalization engine deployment
- Predictive support (anticipate issues before customer contact)

### 3. IT Architecture Modernization

#### Cloud Migration
- **Target:** 75% of workloads on public cloud (AWS/Azure)
- **Timeline:** 18-month migration program
- **Cost Savings:** £15M annual reduction in data center costs

#### API-First Architecture
- 200+ public APIs for partner ecosystem
- TM Forum Open API compliance
- Real-time integration with MVNO partners

### 4. Data & AI/ML Platform

#### Cortex AI Integration
- Deploy Snowflake Cortex for:
  - Churn prediction (85% accuracy target)
  - Network anomaly detection
  - Customer lifetime value forecasting
  - Fraud detection and prevention

#### Data Mesh Architecture
- Domain-oriented data ownership
- Self-serve data infrastructure
- Federated computational governance
