# Subscriber Growth Report Q3 FY2026
## SnowTelco Group PLC - Mobile Subscriber Analytics
### January 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Q3 FY2026 marked a transformational quarter for SnowTelco Consumer Mobile with the achievement of the **1 million subscriber milestone** in December 2025, two months ahead of our original March 2026 target. Net additions of 28,450 subscribers in the quarter brought total subscribers to **1,008,620**, representing 13% year-over-year growth. Monthly churn improved to a record low of 1.02%, while blended ARPU reached £19.94.

## Strategic Insights

1. **Network Quality:** 5G at 79% coverage driving acquisition
2. **Customer Experience:** NPS +42 vs industry +18
3. **Retention Focus:** Proactive upgrade programme highly effective
4. **Digital Channels:** Online cost/acquisition 38% lower than retail
5. **Family Plans:** 22% of base, 25% lower churn

2. **Regional Coverage:** Wales/Scotland below 70%
3. **Business Segment:** Untapped corporate opportunity
4. **Device Attach:** 62% vs target 70%

## Appendix: Key Definitions

- **Net Adds:** Gross additions minus churn
- **Churn Rate:** Churners ÷ Opening subscribers (monthly)
- **ARPU:** Average monthly revenue per user
- **NPS:** Net Promoter Score (% promoters - % detractors)
