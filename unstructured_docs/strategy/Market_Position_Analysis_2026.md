## Executive Summary

SnowTelco Group has achieved a transformational milestone with **1 million mobile subscribers** in December 2025, establishing itself as the UK's leading challenger mobile operator. With ~2.2% market share and the fastest subscriber growth rate in the market, SnowTelco has validated its challenger strategy. In B2B, the company maintains a top 5 position in UK UCaaS with **208,450 Horizon seats** and is the fastest-growing CCaaS provider in the mid-market segment.

SnowTelco has established a strong competitive position in both Consumer Mobile and B2B markets. The one million subscriber milestone validates the challenger mobile strategy, while B2B maintains healthy growth with AI-powered differentiation.

5. Maintain operational excellence
