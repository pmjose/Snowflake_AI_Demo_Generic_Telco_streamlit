## Company Overview

- **SnowTelco Group PLC** - UK telecommunications provider
- **Founded:** 2003, London headquarters
- **CEO:** Sarah Chen
- **Employees:** ~2,400 across UK
- **Listed:** AIM (London Stock Exchange)
- **FY2026 Revenue Target:** £520-540m

---

## Business Segments

### Consumer Mobile (~40% of Revenue)
- **~1 million subscribers** (target March 2026)
- Pay Monthly, SIM Only, and PAYG plans
- UK-focused network coverage
- 5G rollout: 82% population coverage target for FY26
- Monthly churn rate: ~1.1%
- Blended ARPU: ~£17.80
- Competitors: EE, O2, Vodafone, Three

### B2B Communications (~60% of Revenue)

**SnowTelco Connect (UCaaS)**
- Horizon Cloud Phone System
- 185,000+ active seats
- 99.999% platform availability
- Microsoft Teams integration
- Customer NPS: +54

**SnowTelco Experience (CCaaS)**
- Cloud contact centre platform
- ~13,000+ agent seats
- 298 enterprise customers
- AI-powered call analytics
- Workforce management tools

**SnowTelco Enable**
- SIP trunking services
- PSTN replacement solutions
- Connectivity services

**SnowTelco Secure**
- Cyber security services
- Network security
- Growing 20%+ annually
