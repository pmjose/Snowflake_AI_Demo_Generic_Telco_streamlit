# CCO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## SnowTelco UK - Commercial Performance

---

## Persona: Chief Commercial Officer

**Focus Areas:** Revenue growth, sales performance, partner channel, pricing strategy, market expansion

**Semantic Views Used:** SALES_PIPELINE, PARTNER, MOBILE, PORTING, MARKET_INTELLIGENCE

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"As CCO, you're driving SnowTelco's commercial success - from direct sales to partner channels. This platform connects all commercial data, showing you pipeline health, deal performance, and channel effectiveness."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Revenue Dashboard
> "Show me our revenue performance - total revenue by customer type and ARPU trends."

**Expected Insights:**
- Total revenue by customer type (Consumer, SMB, Enterprise)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) by segment
- Revenue by plan type (Contract, SIM Only, PAYG)
- Customer segment distribution (Budget, Standard, Premium, VIP)

**Talking Point:** "Complete commercial visibility - understanding revenue drivers."

---

### Question 2: Sales Pipeline
> "What does our B2B sales pipeline look like? Show me opportunities by stage and expected close dates."

**Expected Insights:**
- Pipeline value by stage (Prospecting through Closed Won)
- Average deal size by vertical
- Opportunity count by lead source
- Quota attainment by team/territory

**Talking Point:** "Pipeline health determines future revenue - we're tracking every opportunity."

---

### Question 3: Channel Performance
> "How are our partner channels performing? Show me partner revenue, commissions, and top performers by tier."

**Expected Insights:**
- Partner revenue by tier (Gold, Silver, Bronze)
- Commission earned by partner
- Top 10 partners by revenue
- Partner NPS scores
- New customers acquired through partners

**Talking Point:** "Partners are critical to our reach - we're investing in the right relationships."

---

### Question 4: Product Mix & Subscriber Analysis
> "What's our subscriber mix? Show me 5G vs 4G adoption, plan types, and ARPU by network generation."

**Expected Insights:**
- Subscriber count by network generation (3G, 4G, 5G)
- Plan type distribution (Pay Monthly, SIM Only, PAYG, Family, Business)
- ARPU comparison: 5G vs 4G subscribers
- Customer segment by plan type

**Talking Point:** "Product mix optimization drives ARPU growth - 5G customers show higher value."

---

### Question 5: Competitive Position
> "How do we compare to competitors? Show me port-in vs port-out by carrier and competitor pricing."

**Expected Insights:**
- Port-in vs port-out volumes by competitor (EE, Vodafone, Three, O2)
- Net port position (winning or losing customers)
- Competitor pricing comparison for similar plans
- Competitor market share trends

**Talking Point:** "Competitive intelligence from real porting data - knowing where we win and lose."

---

## Closing (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"In 10 minutes, you've seen how this platform provides:
- Real-time revenue performance tracking
- Pipeline health and forecasting
- Partner channel management
- Product mix and subscriber analysis
- Competitive position intelligence

Data-driven commercial decisions for growth."
