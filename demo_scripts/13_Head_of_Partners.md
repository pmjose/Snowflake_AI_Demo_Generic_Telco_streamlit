# Head of Partners Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## SnowTelco UK - Partner Channel Management

---

## Persona: Head of Partners / Director of Channel Sales

**Focus Areas:** Partner performance, commission management, recruitment, training, partner experience

**Semantic Views Used:** PARTNER, ORDER, SALES

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"As Head of Partners, you're responsible for SnowTelco's indirect channel - our dealers, resellers, and distribution partners. This platform gives you complete visibility into partner performance, helping you grow the channel and support partner success."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Channel Dashboard
> "Give me an overview of our partner channel - total partners, active this month, and revenue contribution."

**Expected Insights:**
- Total registered partners
- Active partners this month
- Revenue through channel vs direct
- New partner onboardings
- Partner churn rate
- Channel growth trend

**Talking Point:** "Our partner channel is a key growth engine - we're investing in it."

---

### Question 2: Partner Performance
> "Who are our top performing partners this quarter? Show me by revenue, orders, and customer acquisition."

**Expected Insights:**
- Top 10 partners by revenue
- Top 10 by order volume
- Top 10 by new customer adds
- Partner performance by tier (Gold, Silver, Bronze)
- Regional performance breakdown
- Year-over-year growth by partner

**Talking Point:** "Recognizing and rewarding top performers drives channel success."

---

### Question 3: Commission Management
> "What commissions have we paid this month and what's accrued for next payment?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Commission paid this month
- Commission accrued (pending)
- Commission by partner tier
- Commission vs revenue ratio
- Bonus qualification status
- Top earners list

**Talking Point:** "Transparent commission tracking builds partner trust."

---

### Question 4: Partner Health
> "Which partners are underperforming or at risk? Show me activity levels and engagement indicators."

**Expected Insights:**
- Partners with declining sales
- Inactive partners (no orders in 30 days)
- Training completion rates
- Support ticket volume by partner
- NPS scores by partner
- Churn risk indicators

**Talking Point:** "Early intervention helps us retain and grow partner relationships."

---

### Question 5: Recruitment Pipeline
> "What does our partner recruitment pipeline look like? How many new partners have we onboarded?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Recruitment pipeline by stage
- New partners onboarded this quarter
- Time to first sale for new partners
- Partner type distribution
- Geographic coverage gaps
- Competitive recruitment activity

**Talking Point:** "Continuous recruitment expands our reach and market coverage."

---

## Closing (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"In 10 minutes, you've seen how this platform provides:
- Complete channel performance visibility
- Partner ranking and recognition
- Commission management transparency
- Partner health monitoring
- Recruitment pipeline tracking

Partner success drives our success."

---
